### Hey there! 👋

I'm Gabriel Zyur — Creative Developer and 3D Artist.

Whenever I open a web project, the question is always the same: how do I make this unique and authentic? Every project is a chance to build something that didn't exist before — interfaces, experiences, things that have their own identity.

The Blender side is where the other part of me lives: props, environments, worlds built piece by piece into something bigger — the kind of thing that makes a game feel real, and something I genuinely love doing.

At some point, the two paths crossed — and that's when things got really interesting. In that intersection: sometimes it's a 3D detail that brings a project to life, sometimes it's an entire experience built from model to browser. That's exactly what fascinates me — and where I feel at home.
